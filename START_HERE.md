## What These Scripts Do

### `quick_start.sh`
- Downloads your fine-tuned model (Abdine/qwen3-4b-medical-selfplay-sft)
- Installs OpenRLHF (from PyPI)
- Generates 638 training samples
- Verifies local reward function

### `launch_training.sh`
- Checks everything is ready
- Starts training with REINFORCE++
- Uses local judge (all on same GPU)
- Saves checkpoints every 50 steps

---

## What You'll Get

After training completes:

- **Trained models** in `checkpoints/medical_selfplay_RL_<timestamp>/`
- **Attacker model** that introduces realistic medical errors
- **Assessor model** that detects medical errors
- **Both models** improved through self-play

---

## Training Configuration

- **Starting model**: Your fine-tuned Qwen3-4B
- **Training data**: 638 samples (4-way balanced)
- **Algorithm**: REINFORCE++ (from Self-RedTeam paper)
- **Reward**: Zero-sum medical error detection
- **Time**: ~2-4 hours on GPU
